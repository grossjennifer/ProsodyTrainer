Rhythm Reader●

Paste any English text and explore one possible rhythm your mind constructs when you read.

Tap any syllable to move a beat — you know how you read better than any computer does.

Rhythm is fitted across each phrase using strong–weak, weak–strong, weak–weak–strong, and strong–weak–weak feet.

This tool provides a computational approximation of English syllable stress and rhythm. English prosody depends on lexical stress, sentence-level emphasis, phrase structure, discourse context, speaker intention, and individual reading style. It is a reading scaffold, not a rule for how a sentence must be spoken — that's why every syllable is tappable.
For teachers and researchers: the full analysis instrument (three-tier editing, phrase structure, meter detection, research logging, and data export) is in Rhythm Reader Pro.
